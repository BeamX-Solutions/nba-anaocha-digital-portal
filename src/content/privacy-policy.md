### 1. Introduction

The Nigerian Bar Association, Anaocha Branch ("the Branch", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the personal data of its members. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect through the Portal, accessible at **www.nbaanaocha.org.ng**, how we use it, and your rights under the **Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA)**. The Portal is operated on our behalf by **BeamX Solutions Ltd** as data processor.

### 2. Data Controller

**Nigerian Bar Association, Anaocha Branch**
Chief Charles Obegolu Bar Centre, Obeledu, Anaocha LGA, Anambra State, Nigeria
Email: support@nbaanaocha.org.ng

### 3. Data We Collect

**3.1 Identity and Profile Data** — full name, email address, phone number, year of call, office address, and profile photograph.

**3.2 Service Application Data** — information submitted in application forms and uploaded documents (e.g. passport photographs, BPF receipts, branch dues receipts).

**3.3 Dues and Financial Data** — dues status, Paystack payment references, uploaded compliance receipts, and transaction amounts and dates. Card details are never stored by us.

**3.4 Communications Data** — contact form submissions and Portal notifications.

**3.5 Technical Data** — login and session activity, and audit log entries generated by administrative actions on your account.

### 4. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Legal Basis (NDPA 2023) |
|---|---|
| Managing your member account and profile | Contract / Legitimate interests |
| Processing service applications | Contract / Legitimate interests |
| Tracking dues and payments | Contract / Legal obligation |
| Verifying payments with Paystack | Contract |
| Displaying your profile in the member directory | Legitimate interests |
| Sending transactional emails | Contract / Legitimate interests |
| Administrative oversight and audit logging | Legitimate interests / Legal obligation |
| Security monitoring and fraud prevention | Legitimate interests |

### 6. International Data Transfers

Data is stored on Supabase servers in the European Union (France). This constitutes an international transfer under the NDPA 2023, carried out subject to appropriate safeguards including EU GDPR compliance.

### 7. Data Retention

| Data Category | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account and profile data | Duration of membership + 7 years |
| Service application records | 7 years from date of application |
| Payment and transaction records | 7 years |
| Contact form submissions | 2 years |
| Audit logs | 5 years |

### 8. Security

We implement the following measures to protect your personal data:

- Row-Level Security (RLS) ensuring members can only access their own data;
- Encryption in transit via TLS/HTTPS on all data exchanged with the Portal;
- Administrative access restricted to authorised Branch officials; and
- Automated database backups and disaster recovery.

In the event of a breach likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) in accordance with the NDPA 2023.

### 9. Your Rights Under the NDPA 2023

Under the NDPA 2023, you have the right to: access your data; request correction of inaccurate data; request erasure (subject to our legal retention obligations); object to processing; restrict processing; and receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@nbaanaocha.org.ng. We will respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied, you may lodge a complaint with the **Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)** at www.ndpc.gov.ng.

### 10. Cookies and Local Storage

The Portal uses browser local storage and session cookies solely for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

### 11. Children's Data

The Portal is not intended for persons under 18. If you believe a minor's data has been submitted, contact us at support@nbaanaocha.org.ng.

### 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via the Portal or by email. Continued use after the effective date of any update constitutes acceptance.
