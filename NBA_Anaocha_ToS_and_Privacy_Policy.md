### 3. User Accounts and Registration

**3.1** To access the Portal, you must create an account using a valid email address and complete your member profile, including your full name, year of call, phone number, and office address.

**3.4** You may not share your credentials with any other person or use another person's account.

### 4. Services Available on the Portal

The Portal enables members to apply for and track the following Branch services:

- **Annual Practicing Certificate (APC)** — submission of BPF and branch dues receipts for APC processing;
- **Letter of Good Standing** — official letter confirming active membership in good standing;
- **NBA Identification Card** — application for an official NBA ID card;
- **NBA Diary** — ordering of the annual NBA Diary publication;
- **Stamp & Seal** — application for the official NBA Stamp & Seal;
- **Title Document Front Page** — NBA-endorsed front page for title and conveyancing documents; and
- **NBA Vehicle Customized Plate Number** — application for an NBA-customised vehicle plate number.

The availability of any service may be modified or discontinued at the Branch's discretion without prior notice. The Branch does not guarantee processing timescales beyond what is communicated by the Secretariat.

### 5. Dues and Payments

**5.1** The Portal tracks branch dues, levies, and Bar Practicing Fees (BPF) assigned by the Branch. Members are required to fulfil their financial obligations as published in the Portal.

**5.2** All online payments are processed by **Paystack** (Paystack Commerce Inc.), a third-party payment gateway. By making a payment, you also agree to Paystack's terms and conditions. The Branch does not store your card details.

**5.3** Service fees displayed on the Portal at the time of application are the applicable fees. The Branch reserves the right to revise fees at any time; revisions apply to applications made after the date of change.

**5.4** Processing fees are non-refundable once a service request has been accepted. Disputes must be raised with the Secretariat within **14 days** of the transaction date.

**5.5** Uploaded receipts for dues compliance are declarations that the document is genuine and unaltered. Submission of false or forged receipts is a professional misconduct matter and will be referred to the appropriate NBA disciplinary authority.

### 8. Security

We implement the following measures to protect your personal data:

- Row-Level Security (RLS) ensuring members can only access their own data;
- Encryption in transit via TLS/HTTPS on all data exchanged with the Portal;
- Administrative access restricted to authorised Branch officials; and
- Automated database backups and disaster recovery.

In the event of a breach likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) in accordance with the NDPA 2023.

---

### 9. Your Rights Under the NDPA 2023

Under the NDPA 2023, you have the right to: access your data; request correction of inaccurate data; request erasure (subject to our legal retention obligations); object to processing; restrict processing; and receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@nbaanaocha.org.ng. We will respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied, you may lodge a complaint with the **Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)** at www.ndpc.gov.ng.
